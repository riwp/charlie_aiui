# ONE SENTENCE SUMMARY
Optimizing AI expenses involves strategically matching task complexity to specific model capabilities, significantly reducing operational costs and latency without sacrificing the quality of necessary outputs.

# SUMMARY
Interserver explores technical strategies for organizations to slash AI expenditures by 70% through intelligent task-to-model mapping and architectural routing across various LLM performance tiers.
