### SUMMARY
Wilfred Frost interviews Tom Lee of Fundstrat on the Master Investor podcast, discussing his 2026 market outlook, including expected turbulence, long-term investment strategies for equities, energy, basic materials, gold, and cryptocurrencies.

3.  2026 is anticipated to continue the bull market initiated in 2022, showcasing increased economic resilience.

5.  The current bull market is projected to extend for ten years.
6.  The US benefits from a favorable demographic trend with a rising prime-age workforce.
7.  Younger generations (Gen Z, Millennials, Gen Alpha) are poised to inherit substantial wealth, creating wealth disparity.

9.  Major financial institutions are increasingly recognizing blockchain's ability to solve problems and enhance financial services efficiency.

26. Gold functions as an alternative to the dollar or an ultimate backstop currency, rather than a commodity, as its network value vastly exceeds its industrial sales.

30. Ethereum is considered the stronger bull case for crypto, as Wall Street increasingly adopts it for tokenization of assets like dollars, credit funds, and money market funds.
31. If Ethereum's price ratio to Bitcoin recovers to 2021 highs, Ethereum could reach $12,000.
